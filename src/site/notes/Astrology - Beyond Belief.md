### The Human Body & The Solar System

The parallels between the human body and our solar system are something to be in awe of when you start connecting the dots. Consider the following correlations:

- Circadian rhythms synchronize with Earth's 24-hour rotation
- Lunar cycles impact human sleep and emotional patterns
- Lunar cycles effect the tides globally (not directly related to the human body...until you consider the obvious fact that we are primarily composed of water)
- Solar activity has measurable effects on human physiological and psychological states

Mere coincidence, or evidence of our fundamental connection to the heavenly bodies?

After any unbiased consideration we should all at least be able to agree that, based on our body composition alone, the Sun and the Moon have some direct influence over our human experience, internally and externally.

> Our body is a masterpiece of the cosmos. Our body carries within it the stars, the moon, the universe, and the presence of all our ancestors.

-Thich Nhat Hanh

### Zodiac Pie In The Sky

![](https://images.hive.blog/0x0/https://img.inleo.io/DQmXrXzPLsfENBr23dFUWM6s1oPCEmXFQRgdoTjagVwc9mv/heavenly%20law.png)

Next we must address the zodiacs. I would describe the zodiac signs as archetypes that represent different human modes of experience. They are not predetermined destinies, but landscapes that reflect our multifaceted nature.

We previously determined that the sun and the moon have influence over us. If this is a fact, we should also be able to agree that the other planetary bodies within the same solar SYSTEM have to have some level of influence, however minor or significant that may be. Astrology states that the "type" of influence a specific planetary body has on Earth is identifiable. These influences fluctuate based on a particular planets position, composition, magnetism, etc.

Two more things we must consider when discussing the zodiac and its archetypes:

1. These influences or "energies" existed before language. Part of the issue is we recognize the words associated with each planet as the energy itself, instead of an unfolding or manifestation of the energy.
2. There are 2 sides to every coin. Every manifestation has a flip side to it.

Example, there are 4 fundamental energies that make up our reality: fire, air, earth, water. Everything can built on top of, or extracted from these 4 blocks of existence. These 4 elements are also used to classify different zodiac signs. Let's look at the fire signs of Aries, Sagittarius, and Leo (wink wink). The words associated with fire signs are typically confident, independent, passionate, energetic, short-tempered, impatient.

Now we can see that the primordial energy is fire, but it can manifest in a number of different ways while still staying true to its form. It can manifest as the fuel to push you towards yours wildest dreams, or the battery in your back that made you commit a crime of passion.
